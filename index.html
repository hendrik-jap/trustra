Why Trust Trustra™

Built from the inside of actual deals

Not a generic checklist from a blog post. Built by someone who has sat on both sides of the table.

🏛️
Regulatory Precision
Every SG item is mapped to a specific statute — ACRA's Companies Act, IRAS requirements, MAS licensing rules, MOM work pass obligations. Not generalised compliance, but the exact documents regulators and investors look for.
📐
Scoring With Institutional Logic
Category weights are assigned based on where investors actually spend their time in each deal type. Cap table and financials dominate Series A. Legal and tax are heavier in M&A. SGX adds governance and prospectus layers. It mirrors the real process.
🎯
Critical Items Are Flagged, Not Buried
Missing an IP assignment or a vesting schedule can kill a deal even when everything else is in order. These deal-breakers are highlighted so founders fix the right things first — not just fill a checklist from top to bottom.
🔒
Confidentiality by Architecture
Your company's preparation is commercially sensitive. Trustra™ runs entirely in the browser — no backend, no analytics on your answers, no cloud storage. Share results only when you choose to, via a URL you control.

About the Creator

Hendrik Jap

FCCA CA (Singapore) CFO, Kacamatamoo Group

Hendrik is a finance executive operating at the intersection of strategy, compliance, and capital markets. As CFO of Kacamatamoo — a vision care group with over 100 locations across ASEAN — he leads pre-IPO readiness, FP&A, and ESG for a business scaling fast across multiple jurisdictions.

He serves as an Independent Director on the boards of several Singapore-incorporated firms, advising on sustainability, compliance, AI adoption, and finance transformation at various stages of growth.

He guest lectures at SMU on venture capital mechanics and has been featured twice in ACCA's AB Magazine — on the fractional CFO model in APAC, and on the rise of finance leadership in high-growth emerging market businesses. He was also invited to speak at the FutureCFO conference on sustainability and AI.
